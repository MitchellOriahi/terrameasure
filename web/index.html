What is land surveying — and why doesn’t this need equipment? Traditional land surveying requires licensed professionals, GPS receivers, total stations, and weeks of scheduling. TerraMeasure skips all of that: we read the same satellite elevation data that government agencies publish for free, and run the measurements automatically in your browser. How it works

Three steps. No equipment.

From picking a location to a full report in under a minute.

1

Pick any location on Earth

Click anywhere on the interactive map to choose your survey area. Draw a point or a polygon around the land you want to measure. No GPS device. No site visit.

2

We fetch the elevation data

We automatically pull real government elevation data for your exact location — the same satellite measurements used by the US Geological Survey. Nothing for you to download, install, or collect.

3

Get your results instantly

Slope, area, elevation profile, buildability score, and flood & fire risk — all calculated and displayed in seconds. No waiting. No download. No equipment needed at any step.
